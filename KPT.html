How to make Kueh Pie Tee


Instructions

  1. Blanch shrimps and set aside.
  2. Blanch beansprouts and set aside.
  3. Add eggs into a pot of water and bring to a boil.
    Once boiling, cook for about 7 minutes to achieve hard boiled eggs with a creamy centre.
    Remove eggs from water and peel off shell. Roughly chop eggs and set aside.
  4. Pan fry beancurd chunks until crispy on all sides. Cut into smaller cubes and set aside.
  5. Add about 1-2 tbsp of oil into frypan and saute garlic and shallots until fragrant.

This is Nyonya Kuih Pie Tee or also known as Top Hats. It's an appetizer made with a savory, crispy thin fried shell, filled with stir-fry yam bean and carrot.
